Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, you may return most items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

Eligible Returns

Items must be:

  • Unworn, unwashed, and in original condition
  • With all original tags attached
  • In original packaging (if applicable)
  • Returned within 30 days of delivery

Non-Returnable Items

  • Digital Products: All digital downloads and content are final sale
  • Signed/Personalized Items: Custom signed merchandise cannot be returned
  • Intimate Apparel: For hygiene reasons, certain items are final sale
  • Sale Items: Items marked as final sale or clearance
  • Gift Cards: Non-refundable

How to Return

  1. Request a Return
    Email returns@wyattxxxcole.xxx with your order number and reason for return.
  2. Receive Return Label
    We'll send you a prepaid return shipping label (US orders only).
  3. Ship Your Return
    Pack items securely and drop off at any carrier location.
  4. Receive Refund
    Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days of receiving your return.

Exchanges

Want a different size or color? The fastest way is to return your item for a refund and place a new order. This ensures you get your new item as quickly as possible.

Damaged or Defective Items

Received a damaged or defective item? Contact us immediately at support@wyattxxxcole.xxx with photos of the issue. We'll send a replacement or process a refund right away.

International Returns

International customers are responsible for return shipping costs. We recommend using a trackable shipping method. Original shipping charges and any customs/duties are non-refundable.

Refund Method

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method. Please allow 5-10 business days for the refund to appear on your statement after processing.

Questions?

Our support team is here to help! Email us at support@wyattxxxcole.xxx or use the contact form.
